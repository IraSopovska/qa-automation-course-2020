Test Scenario:

1. Verify the user has the ability to search the information on the website ( search functionality)
2. Verify the user has the ability to open the facebook version of the sity
3. Verify the ability to find the company contacts
4. Verify the user can view archival news/articles
5. Verify the user has the ability to post comments under the articles/news

Test Cases for Test Scenario: Verify the user has the ability to post comments under the articles/news

1. Go to https://pravda.if.ua
2. Click on one of breaking news on the Main page
3. Scroll to the bottom of the page

2.Check the presense of text editing panel in the comment section

Steps:
1. Go to https://pravda.if.ua
2. Click on one of breaking news on the Main page
3. Scroll to the bottom of the page

3.Check the behavior when the Nickname is entered

3. Scroll to the bottom of the page
4. Entered the comment
5. Entered the Nickname
